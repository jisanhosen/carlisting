WHAT IS VIHO CLUB

Saint John Hyundai treats the needs of each individual customer with paramount concern. We know that you have high expectations, and as a car dealer we enjoy the challenge of meeting and exceeding those standards each and every time! The VIHO club demonstrates our commitment to excellence. Our experienced sales staff are eager to share their knowledge and enthusiasm with you. Call anytime with any questions or concerns, they are here to help you!

Stop in anytime for a coffee - we welcome you to our home at 403 Westmorland Road anytime that you are in the neighbourhood! Our goal is to offer you our continued support long after the sale through service, check-ups and, of course, the VIHO club. We want you to know how valued and important you are to us, and through the VIHO club you will feel like a VIP at businesses all over the city ! You know you are saving money and buying peace of mind when it comes to our quality vehicles, and we want you to continue saving all year long, not only in our service department, but at the best of the best businesses - including everything from food and salons to golf and lawyers! We hand chose each business based on their reputation and quality of service - we know you will be pleased and well taken care of at each establishment!

This is your VIHO Club business directory to allow you to view the many amazing businesses where you will receive VIP status by way of a product discount! This is just our way of saying THANK YOU for choosing Saint John Hyundai, and enjoy your quality vehicle!

Becoming a member means that you save big dollars all over Saint John, at businesses that you shop at everyday! We are redefining 'Customer Service' and want our customers to know that we are looking after them LONG after they purchase a car.

We want our customers to proudly tell their friends that they saved on gas, or that they are 'VIP-in' tonight at East Side Marios. We want them to laugh in their neighbour's face when he complains about the price of gas. We want them to wave their VIHO VIP Club card at their co-workers when they pay full price for lunch, and our customer, of course, does not.

Because when Hyundai customers' smile, so do we. And when they brag about their car, AND their dealer....well, we brag right along with them.

Do you want to save $$ all over town?
Join the Club. The VIHO Club.
